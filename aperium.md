# The Magic Safety Commission (or Aperium)
The Magic Safety Commission is an international organization forseeing magic regulation across continents. It is their job to make sure governments and individuals abide to the law so that no international magic feuds happen. The Commission employs more than 10,000 people all over the world with 100 different divisions. Within the Commissions there are a few roles:
- The Dean of Commission is the person seeing over the entire organization.
- Lawmakers and Research Magicians work together to edit and create laws.
- Enforcers are in charge of carrying out the regulation once the entity shows signs of breaking the laws
- Ambassadors are sent to governments to discuss policies
## Magical Artifact Retrieving Division
Also called "the fetchers" for short, this division is in charge of locating and retrieving lost high-grade artifacts. In case the artifacts' owner has passed away or doesn't want the artifact, it is also their duty to seal, destroy, or bring the artifact back to Aperium's headquarter for safekeeping.
Known members:
